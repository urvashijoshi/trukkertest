ABOUT TruKKer LITE

TruKKer Lite is a revolutionary product that provides transporters with the ability to bring in the touch of technology into their business and make data-driven business decisions for FREE. It will enable the registered transporter to manage their trucks and drivers while having the ability to track them without fitting GPS devices on trucks, ensuring FREE GPS TRACKING WITHOUT ANY INVESTMENT.

Moreover, it enables them to manage operations such as allocating a truck & driver, adding a truck and its driver from anywhere through our application. Thus, it provides a FLEET MANAGEMENT SOLUTION FOR FREE

Also, Lite strives to help the truck drivers deal with routine issues such as the inability to determine an address when in city limits, continuous follow-ups; mix-ups in deliveries etc. Drivers will also earn TruKKer loyalty points for using the App.

WHY TruKKer LITE

Stress Free

Manage trucks, driver and operations while reducing unwanted paperwork and chaos that is usually part of the transportation industry. Moreover real time tracking reduces status inquiries from receivers and shippers.

Simple

Have all operations in one place for transporter and driver through a user friendly interface that allows swift allocation of trucks and drivers for a load request, while reflecting the same information to the driver in real time.

Trustworthy

Real time tracking and the ability to share the tracking link not only strengthens the trust of the shipper and receiver, but also assures the transporter of the current status of a load request. Moreover, the OTP option ensures quick Point of Delivery confirmation that the consignment is received by the intended authorized party.
